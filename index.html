About

In Philosophy I dedicate myself to study and build theories mainly related to whistle-blowing, cyberactivism, posmodernism, information society, new medias, cybernetics and aesthetics.

As a journalist, I've been working on analysing public leaked documents in order shine a light on human rights violations, corruption and global lobbies. I'm currently one of the editors of the Associated Whistle-Blowing Press.

I also perform some sort of Arts, mainly focused on political awareness, social criticism and enhancement of freedom through urban and online interventions. I've been writing poetry since the age of 13.

I speak in events worldwide about all those topics listed above.
